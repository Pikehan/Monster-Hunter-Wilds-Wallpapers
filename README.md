I really love Monster Hunter and always wanted some good mobile wallpapers, but I couldn't find decent ones formatted for phone screen ratios. 

So, I booted up Monster Hunter Wilds and took some vertical 4K screenshots of my favorite moments from the cutscenes. The screenshots are shot in a 9:16 ratio, but I framed them specifically for 19.5:9 *(which is the most common mobile phone aspect ratio)*. I shot versions both with and without the in-game vignette.

---

Because my in-game gamma is set pretty low, the blacks are a bit crushed and the overall images are a bit dark. I wanted to touch them up, but I am bad at photo editing. Still, I lightly edited them on my phone so they aren't too dark when actually used as wallpapers. 

---

I've shared two folders here:

* **Edited files:** For people who just want to download them and don't wanna mess with editing.
* **Original screenshots:** The raw files for people who want to do their own, better image editing.

---

I would be happy if you do stylistic edits and share it with everyone.

*I am also planning to create desktop versions of these wallpapers formatted for 16:9, ultrawide, and dual-monitor setups.*

**Happy Hunting!**
